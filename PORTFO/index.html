About me

"I'm Alia Bhatt, a Bollywood actress born and raised in the heart of Mumbai.Acting isn't just a career for me; it's a passion that runs deep in my veins. You might remember me from films like 'Student of the Year' and 'Raazi,' where I've had the privilege to bring diverse characters to life on the silver screen. But beyond the glitz and glamour of the industry, I'm just a girl with dreams, navigating my way through life's adventures. Whether it's immersing myself in a challenging role or advocating for causes close to my heart, I strive to make a meaningful impact wherever I go.

And hey, fashion isn't just a hobby; it's an expression of my personality. From red carpet glam to casual chic, I love experimenting with different styles and trends.

But perhaps what truly drives me is the opportunity to inspire others, especially the youth of our nation. Through my work and actions, I hope to encourage others to chase their dreams fearlessly and make a positive difference in the world. So, here's to embracing every moment, chasing every dream, and living life unapologetically. Because for me, the journey is just as important as the destination."

Sadak 2

2020-Action,Drama


The film picks up where Sadak left off, revolving around the journey with a young girl who becomes an important character in the life of the protagonist, and the reason for him to stay alive.

Sadak 2 is a 2020 Indian Hindi-language action thriller road film directed by Mahesh Bhatt and produced by Fox Star Studios and Mukesh Bhatt under their banner Vishesh Films. A sequel to the 1991 film Sadak, it stars Sanjay Dutt (reprising his role from the original), Alia Bhatt and Aditya Roy Kapur in the lead roles with an ensemble supporting cast, while Pooja Bhatt makes a special appearance.

Dear Zindagi

2016-Drama Romance


Kaira is a budding cinematographer in search of a perfect life. Her encounter with Jug, an unconventional thinker, helps her gain a new perspective on life. She discovers that happiness is all about finding comfort in life's imperfections.

Dear Zindagi is a 2016 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age comedy drama film written and directed by Gauri Shinde. It was produced by Gauri Khan, Karan Johar, and Shinde under the banners of Red Chillies Entertainment, Dharma Productions, and Hope Productions, respectively.

Gully Boy

2019-Drama,Music


A coming-of-age story based on the lives of street rappers in Mumbai..
Gully Boy is a 2019 Indian Hindi-language musical drama film directed by Zoya Akhtar, and written by Akhtar and Reema Kagti. The film was produced by Ritesh Sidhwani, Akhtar and Farhan Akhtar under the banners of Tiger Baby Films and Excel Entertainment productions.
